📊 Why Financial PR is Different

A crisis for a public company can wipe billions off market cap. Unlike consumer brand PR, financial PR directly impacts shareholder trust and stock price stability.

What is Financial PR?

Financial PR is the specialized practice of public relations for listed companies, involving investor relations, media relations, and crisis management to protect and enhance market capitalization.

IPO-Stage Financial PR

12 Months Before IPO: Foundation

Establish IR framework, prepare press release templates, train executives

6 Months Before IPO: Market Warm-up

Pre-IPO news, manage media expectations, build analyst relations

Roadshow: Full Sprint

Executive roadshow, media interviews, institutional investor meetings

Post-Listing: Ongoing Maintenance

Regular earnings, analyst briefings, crisis preparedness
