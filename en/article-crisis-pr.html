What is Crisis PR?

Crisis PR is the strategic management of communication during a crisis situation. It involves rapid response, transparent communication, and reputation recovery.

The 4-Hour Rule

Studies show that companies who respond within 4 hours of a crisis see 80% less reputational damage than those who wait longer.

Crisis Response Timeline

0-1 Hour: Crisis Detection & Assessment

Monitor social media, news, and forums. Assess severity and potential impact.

1-4 Hours: Rapid Response

Issue initial statement acknowledging the situation. Prepare facts and key messages.

4-24 Hours: Active Management

Deploy comprehensive response strategy. Engage with media and stakeholders.

24-72 Hours: Recovery Phase

Shift from defense to proactive communication. Highlight positive actions taken.

Week 2+: Reputation Rebuilding

Long-term positioning, thought leadership, and positive news cycle.
