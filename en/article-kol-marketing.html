What is KOL Marketing?

KOL (Key Opinion Leader) marketing leverages influential individuals to promote products and brand messages to their engaged audiences.

Unlike Western influencer marketing, Chinese KOLs operate across multiple platforms and often have deeper, more trusting relationships with followers.
